Ask for Help

The easiest way to ask for help with DroidEdit is to contact the developer directly at droideditapp@gmail.com.

Please bear in mind that DroidEdit is developed by only one person as a pet project. I might not have time to answer all your questions but they are all taken into consideration.

Report a Bug

To report a bug or request a new feature for DroidEdit, please use droidedit.com/support.
