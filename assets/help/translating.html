Current Translations

Thanks to a great group of volunteers (too many to mention), DroidEdit has been translated into several labguages.

Currently you can use DroidEdit in the following languages: English, Czech, German, Spanish, French, Hungarian, Japanese, Polish, Portuguese (PT and BR), Russian, Swedish and Chinese.

Helping

If you want your own language to be supported by DroidEdit or just want to correct a translation error, you can go to www.getlocalization.com/droidedit/ and start helping out.
